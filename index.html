About

A mind built
for both worlds

I grew up creating — sketching, building, imagining things that didn't exist yet. That instinct led me to study Computer Science with a software engineering focus, where I discovered that the most compelling interfaces are those where logic becomes invisible and beauty does the talking.

Graphic design became my language. I use it to translate complex ideas into visual systems that feel effortless — because true craft conceals its own effort. My technical background means I don't just design beautifully; I design with an understanding of how things actually get built.

I'm fluent in both Spanish and English (C1 Advanced), which means I bring the same precision to communication as I do to composition.
